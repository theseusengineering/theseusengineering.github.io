Custom 3D Design Services

Have a unique idea in mind? We offer bespoke design services to turn your concepts into reality.

  • Fill out the form, and we will be in contact to brainstorm and discuss your requirements.
  • We create a custom 3D model based on your specifications, and then print and deliver it to your door.
  • DISCLAIMER: 3D Prints that need to interact with everyday objects (eg. remote, pens) are very dependant on exact measurements. If you do not have calipers or you are unconfident with your measurements, we offer in-person digital scanning/measurements. There is a chance that if we only use your measurements, it may not fit upon first try. Any remodelling and redesigning will incur appropriate charges within reason. We offer free remodels and redesigns when using our in-person bespoke measurements.
